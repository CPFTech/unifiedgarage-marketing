Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Note: This is a placeholder privacy policy for our beta period. We'll replace it with a full policy reviewed by counsel before general availability. If you have questions in the meantime, email privacy@unifiedgarage.com.

1. What we collect

When you sign up for or use UnifiedGarage, we collect information you provide directly (name, email, dealership details, billing information) and information generated by your use of the service (inventory records, deal data, service records, usage logs, IP address, device/browser info).

2. How we use it

We use your information to operate the service, process transactions, communicate with you about your account, improve the product, detect and prevent fraud or abuse, and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell your personal information.

3. How we share it

We share information only with service providers who help us operate UnifiedGarage (hosting, payments, email delivery, error monitoring, analytics), when required by law, or with your consent. All providers are bound by confidentiality obligations.

4. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period afterwards to meet legal, tax, and operational requirements. You may request deletion at any time per Section 7.

5. Security

We use industry-standard security practices, including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, role-based access controls, and regular security reviews. No system is perfectly secure, but we take this seriously.

6. Cookies and tracking

We use essential cookies to keep you signed in and to remember your preferences. We may use analytics cookies to understand product usage. You can disable non-essential cookies via your browser settings.

7. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information, restrict or object to certain processing, and lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. To exercise these rights, email privacy@unifiedgarage.com.

8. Children

UnifiedGarage is a business product not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. International users

Your information may be processed and stored in the United States and other countries where we or our service providers operate. We use appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll post the new version with a revised "last updated" date, and for material changes we'll notify you by email.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email privacy@unifiedgarage.com.
