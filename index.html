What's inside

Every department. One login.

Sales, service, parts, F&I, BDC, and accounting all work out of the same system — because they work with each other every day.

Inventory & recon

Every unit from landed to front line in one queue. Aging alerts, photo capture, VIN decode, book values, market-based pricing, and recon step tracking.

Deal desking & F&I

Work multiple payment scenarios side by side, pass to F&I without re-keying, present a menu the customer understands, and post to the GL when it funds.

Service & parts

Write ROs, dispatch techs, track hours, check part availability, and get customer approval by text before any wrench turns.

Sales CRM & BDC

Round-robin lead assignment, call/text/email tracking, be-back reminders, and a pipeline that shows who's hot and who's ghosting.

Reports that help

Units desked, turn time, gross per copy, F&I penetration, inventory aging, tech efficiency, CSI — filter, export, pin to dashboard.

Customer portal

Customers see what they bought, service history, and upcoming recalls — on their phone, branded with your store.

Accounting & GL

Deal posting, month-end, bank recs, and a chart of accounts purpose-built for dealer schedules. Not generic small-business bookkeeping.

Multi-rooftop

Run three stores or thirty. Share customers across rooftops, transfer inventory, see group-level reports, scope users per store.

Trade-in appraisals

Condition grading, multiple book values, manager bump-approval, one-click attachment to the deal jacket. No more lost forms.
