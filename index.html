What's inside

Every department. One login.

Sales, service, parts, F&I, BDC, and accounting all work out of the same system — because they work with each other every day.

Inventory & Recon

Every unit from landed to front line in one queue. Aging alerts, photo capture, VIN decode, book values, market-based pricing, and recon step tracking — no more whiteboards or spreadsheets.

Deal Desking & F&I

Work multiple payment scenarios side-by-side, pass the deal to F&I without re-keying a thing, present a menu the customer actually understands, and post straight to the GL when it funds.

Service & Parts

Write ROs, dispatch techs, track hours, check part availability, and get customer approval by text before any wrench turns. Service writers, techs, and parts clerks finally share one screen.

Sales CRM & BDC

Round-robin lead assignment, call/text/email tracking, be-back reminders, and a pipeline that shows who's hot and who's ghosting. Ups, leads, be-backs — all in one queue your floor actually checks.

Reports that actually help

Units desked, turn time, gross per copy, F&I penetration, inventory aging, tech efficiency, CSI — filter by salesperson, day, or rooftop. Export to Excel or pin to your dashboard.

Customer Portal

Your customers see what they bought, their service history, pending work, and upcoming recalls — on their phone, branded with your store. Service appointments and e-signatures included.

Accounting & GL

Deal posting, month-end, bank recs, and a real dealership chart of accounts — purpose-built for car deals, parts inventory, and service schedules. Not generic small-business bookkeeping.

Multi-Rooftop

Run three stores or thirty. Share customers across rooftops, transfer inventory between stores, see group-level reports, and scope every user to the store(s) they belong to.

Trade-In Appraisals

Structured appraisal form with condition grading, photos, multiple book values, manager bump-approval, and one-click attachment to the deal jacket. No more appraisal forms disappearing into someone's desk.
